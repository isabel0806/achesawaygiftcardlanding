FAQ

Common Questions

Do gift cards expire?
No. Aches Away gift cards never expire — the recipient can book whenever they're ready, no pressure.
Can I add a personal message?
Yes! At checkout you can add a custom message that gets included with the gift card delivery.
How does the recipient book their sessions?
They'll receive their gift card by email with a unique code. They use it to book directly through Aches Away's Jane App booking system online.
Are bundles covered by insurance?
Standard RMT sessions within bundles may be covered depending on the recipient's plan. Because bundles are sold as gift cards (not direct billing), we recommend checking with their provider. The receipt can be provided upon request.
Can the recipient choose their therapist?
Yes — when booking online, they can select their preferred therapist based on availability and specialty.
